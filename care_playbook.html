Complaint Handling

🚚 Delivery Issues

Standard operating procedure for handling delivery-related complaints. All cases require IRD team verification before resolution.

📦

1.1 Short Item Delivered

1
Complaint Generation IB team generates complaint and escalates ticket to Resolution team.
2
Verification Check order details, SKU Name, and Disputed Quantity with customer over call.
3
Escalation Escalate over Slack and G Sheet (Customer Claims Database – National) with IRD team to verify customer claim.
4
Verdict IRD team shares verdict in the sheet. Video obtained if necessary and cross-checked.
5
Customer History Check Review complaints history and historic returns before providing resolution.
6
Rider History Check Review rider's complaint history before providing resolution.

✅ If Valid

  • Refund via coins if stock value < PKR 500
  • Place a 0-value order for high-value items
  • Update customer on resolution to close loop

📋 Note

  • Resolution depends on IRD verdict
  • Park the shortage accordingly
🔄

1.2 Wrong Item Delivered

1
Complaint Generation IB team generates complaint with proof (picture or video).
2
Verification Check order details, SKU Name, Disputed Qty with customer over call.
3
Escalation Escalate complaint with difference amount over Slack and G Sheet with IRD team.
4
Verdict + History Checks IRD shares verdict. Check customer and rider complaint history.

✅ If Valid

  • Refund difference amount via coins if stock < PKR 500
  • Place RL order for high-value items
  • Update customer on resolution

📋 Note + IRD Verdict

  • Resolution depends on IRD verdict; park shortage accordingly
  • Resolve according to customer's preference
  • If customer not satisfied with coins, facilitate through coins alternatively
  • Shaheen Error: RL order delivered to customer and PO generated to balance inventory
➕

1.3 Extra Item Delivered

1
Complaint Generation IB team generates complaint with proof shared by customer (picture or video).
2
Verification Check order details, SKU Name, Extra Qty with customer over call.
3
Escalation Escalate complaint with video proof over Slack and G Sheet with IRD team.
4
Verdict IRD team shares verdict in the sheet.

✅ Low-Value (Below PKR 500)

  • Let customer keep as goodwill
  • Appreciate customer honesty when closing loop

📦 High-Value Items

  • Arrange pickup if feasible

📋 IRD Verdict Breakdown

  • Dispatched Extra (below 500): Customer keeps as goodwill
  • Dispatched Extra (above 500): Arrange pickup after aligning customer
  • LM Error: Align rider with customer to settle payment; shortage logged against Shaheen

⚡ Auto Resolution

Criteria for automatically resolving Ghar (Household) customer delivery & quality complaints — based on customer type, payment mode, promo usage, proxy status, and complaint history.

🗺️

Decision Tree — Ghar Customer Profiling

🎯

Eligibility Criteria (from SQL)

Derived from final_base → eligible_for_auto_resolve & max_dispute_amount_allowed

🚫 Always Escalate — NOT eligible for auto-resolve

Condition 1: Customer used a Promo/Voucher (any order in last 6 months)
promo_flag = 'Promo'
Condition 2: FTO customer who is a Proxy
customer_type = 'FTO' AND is_proxy = 'Yes'
Condition 3: Returning customer with ≥ 3 escalated complaints in last 2 months
customer_type ≠ 'FTO' AND escalated_complaints_last_2m ≥ 3

✅ Auto-Resolve Tiers

Customer Type Proxy? Promo? Complaints (last 2M) Payment Max Dispute Cap Action
FTO No No Any Prepaid PKR 500 Auto-resolve
FTO No No Any Postpaid (Cash) PKR 500 Auto-resolve
Returning — No ≤ 1 Prepaid PKR 1000 Auto-resolve
Returning — No = 2 Prepaid PKR 500 Auto-resolve
Returning — No ≥ 3 Any — Escalate
Returning — No Any Postpaid (Cash) — Escalate
Any — Yes Any Any — Escalate
FTO Yes No Any Any — Escalate
💡

Resolution Method — When Auto-Resolved

When a complaint is eligible for auto-resolve and the dispute value is within the cap, resolution is offered on customer's choice:

🪙 Option A — Coins Refund

Disputed amount credited to customer's Bazaar Wallet as coins. Instant, no logistics required.

🔄 Option B — Reverse Logistics (RL)

Pickup arranged for wrong/damaged item and correct item dispatched or refund issued after pickup.

📖

Key Definitions from SQL

FieldHow It's Computed
customer_type = 'FTO'First order within the last 15 days AND total booked orders = 1
is_proxy = 'Yes'Store ID found in Mixpanel + Google Analytics cross-match (device advertising ID match in last 13 days)
promo_flag = 'Promo'Any order in last 6 months with discount_type = 'VOUCHER'
payment_flag = 'Prepaid'Any payment mode includes CARD, DIGITAL_PAYMENT, or WALLET in last 6 months
escalated_complaints_last_2mCount of distinct escalated issue_numbers in last 2 months (customer support issues, excluding self-resolved)
Eligible issuesCovers: Short/wrong/extra delivery, late delivery, damaged, expired, weight issue, quality, MRP, cash shortage, agent behaviour, etc.
Lookup window6 months for order history; 2 months for complaint check; 13 days for proxy detection
⚠️ TAT Breach Exception Any case with a TAT breach (order delayed beyond committed SLA) is automatically escalated regardless of the above criteria — it bypasses the auto-resolve flow entirely.

Communication flow for delayed orders due to weather or operational reasons. Four templates covering different delay scenarios.

📌 Apology Coins Rule (General)

Default rule unless stated otherwise: 5% of order GMV, minimum PKR 100, maximum PKR 500.

1. b2c Proactive Update Before Delay

No Image
Order rescheduled due to weather or operational delays
5% of GMV · Min 100 · Max 500
Inform customer of new delivery date, assure transparency, remind about apology coins.
Sample Message
Hi [Cst Name], Due to heavy rains in your area, your order ([#Order_No]) might be delayed today. We are closely monitoring the situation and will keep you updated. You can always check the latest status in the order details on the app. Thank you for your patience and trust in us. 💙

2. b2c Rescheduled Delivery

🖼️ Image A
When delay is anticipated before the promised delivery time
5% of GMV · Min 100 · Max 500
Empathetic apology, inform in advance, mention apology coins.
Sample Message
Hi [Cst Name], We're really sorry your order ([#Order_No]) cannot be delivered today due to the weather. We are rescheduling and will confirm your new delivery date soon. As a small token of apology, we have added PKR [ApologyCoins] worth of coins to your wallet. You can view updates in the order details on the app. If you have any questions, just tap below, we're always here to help! 💙 Thank you for your patience and trust in us.

3. b2c Delay Confirmation with Apology

🖼️ Image A
When order has already been delayed beyond committed slot
5% of GMV · Min 100 · Max 500
Apology, confirm new timeline, highlight apology coins for compensation.

Just a reminder, we have also added apology coins (PKR [ApologyCoins]) in your account. In case of any further changes, we'll let you know in advance. Meanwhile, you can check the updated delivery time in your order details on the app. Thank you for waiting and for your continued trust in us. 💙

4. b2c Delivery Confirmation

🖼️ Image B
After the order has been successfully delivered
Not applicable (no coins)
Confirmation of successful delivery, thank customer for trust.

↩️ Returns, Refunds & Exchanges

Return/exchange policy, eligible items, timelines, and refund process.

⏱️ 48-Hour Rule

All complaints must be reported within 48 hours of receiving the order via the Bazaar WhatsApp helpline. Requests after 48 hours are not eligible — though we still log the ticket.

What is Bazaar's return and exchange policy? ▼
What is your return policy? Can I exchange an item? How long do I have to report an issue?
All complaints must be reported within 48 hours of receiving your order through the Bazaar WhatsApp helpline. Requests after 48 hours are not eligible for refund or exchange. Items can be exchanged if you received a wrong, faulty, expired, or damaged item — or if you ordered by mistake.
🔒 Agent Note Category: Returns & Refunds → Return/Exchange Policy | Tags: return_policy, exchange_policy, 48hr_rule | If complaint older than 48 hours → inform customer but still log ticket
Which items CANNOT be returned or exchanged? ▼
What items are not returnable? Can I return tobacco or imported items? Is HPC exchangeable?
The following categories are not eligible for return or exchange:
• Tobacco / Nicotine products
• Promotional or sale items
• Health & Personal Care (HPC)
• Imported goods
• Pet supplies

📍 Order Delivery, Tracking & Updates

How to track orders, delivery notifications, delay complaints, and rider contact.

How can I check my delivery status? ▼
Where is my order? Rider kahan tak pohoncha? Status not visible
Bazaar offers next-day delivery. On the delivery day, track your order in the app:

1. Open the App
2. Go to Account → Orders
3. Select your Dispatched Order

You'll see a 3-hour delivery window once your order is dispatched. The rider will contact you on your registered number before arriving. If the delivery window isn't visible, your order hasn't been dispatched yet. "All orders" shows confirmed but not-yet-dispatched orders and all previously delivered orders.
🔒 Agent Note Category: Delivery → Tracking | Tags: order_status, delivery_window
Will you notify me about my order? ▼
Yes, Bazaar sends push notifications whenever there is an update to your order's delivery status. Make sure push notifications are enabled for the Bazaar app.

🏦 Credit Lending Facility

Bazaar offers a lending/credit facility to selected retail customers only — this is not a credit card.

⚠️ Important: This facility is NOT a credit card. It is a lending facility provided at the company's discretion based on order history, business activity, and relationship with Bazaar.

Who is eligible for Bazaar Credit? ▼
How can I get credit on Bazaar? Am I eligible for credit? Why don't I have the credit option?
Bazaar offers a credit facility only to selected retail customers. Eligibility depends on factors like your order history, business activity, repayment record, and overall relationship with Bazaar. This facility is provided entirely at the company's discretion and is not available for all customers.
🔒 Agent Note Category: Lending / Credit → Eligibility | Tags: credit_eligibility, selective_credit | If customer insists they should be eligible → create "Credit Eligibility Check" ticket
Is the credit facility available in my city? ▼
The credit facility is available in select cities and to select customers only. Availability is determined by Bazaar at its discretion. Contact support to check if you are eligible in your area.
